Street Foods

Philippine street foods are a vibrant mix of flavors and traditions, featuring iconic snacks like fish balls, isaw (grilled chicken intestines), and kwek-kwek (battered quail eggs). Often served on skewers or in small portions, these delicious bites reflect the country’s rich culinary heritage and are enjoyed by locals and tourists alike. Perfect for quick meals, they offer a taste of Filipino culture in every bite.
